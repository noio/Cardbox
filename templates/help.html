Lists

Each List contains any number of related words. The most obvious example is a vocabulary from a chapter of a book. You'll only have to enter these words once, then you can re-use them any way you like.

Words are entered in a simple format, like so:

columns: [dutch, en, pos]
rows:
 - [fiets, bicycle, noun]
 - [kaas, cheese, noun]

Templates

A Template defines the look of your card! Colors, fonts, sizes, position, all of those are set in a template. You don't have to worry about them too much though, just pick one.

Cardsets

When you combine a list of words with a template, you get cards! When you create or edit a cardset, simply select a List and a Template, and decide which words should go where on the front and the back of the card.

Boxes

Your progress is kept in a Box. In each box, you can put any number of Cardsets and study those! When you start studying, the cards that you have memorized least well will be presented to you.

You could have a separate box for each language you are learning or separate boxes for each semester, it's up to you.
